The Lakou Mizik album will unite generations of musicians spanning musical genres to re-create classic Haitian songs with new lyrics, rhythms & creative production.

Through collaboration with internationally known producers, the music will have instant cross over appeal to a global audience.

Much as Buena Vista Social Club did for Cuba, Lakou Mizik will introduce the world to Haiti's important musical heritage while supporting its continued artistic evolution.
