Lakou Mizik is a social change platform using Haiti's powerful cultural assets to encourage engagement in its economic & social development by:

  • Supporting Haiti's creative economy through an online marketplace that connects Haitian musicians to international audiences and distribution networks
  • Providing local musicians & producers access to technology, donated instruments, and educational programs
  • Working with musicians to identify social issues affecting their communities, and with local organizations implementing sustainable solutions
  • Creating a replicable model for promoting the unique cultural products of economically disadvantaged communities
