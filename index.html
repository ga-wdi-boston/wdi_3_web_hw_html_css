Musings on Modest Mouse

The anger-, sadness-, confusion-, lament-, and weirdness-filled music of Modest Mouse has always touched me in a way that's difficult to describe. This site voices my appreciation for some favorite lyrics, riffs and melodies from what I consider to be the band's best work.
