Harding Hill Farm

Harding Hill Farm is a 3rd generation farm that has been managing and harvesting forest lands in a sustainable manner for over 50 years. In addition to timber harvesting and forest management, Harding Hill Farm makes maple syrup, harvests about 5,000 square bales of hay, and raises a small herd of beef cattle. HHF now provides tree and shrub care, which includes pruning, removals, and cabling. Harding Hill Farm has on staff a NH Licensed Forester, a NH Certified Arborist, and two Certified Professional Loggers.a
