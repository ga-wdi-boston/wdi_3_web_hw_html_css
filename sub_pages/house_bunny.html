Rabbits as Pets

Everyone's heard of rabbits; cute, energetic bundles of fun and playfulness. But the house rabbit is a fad that's only just started. It's one of the many ways to care for your rabbit - it will be treated like one of the family. All rabbits have a life expectancy of 5-12 years. They can run around the whole house, and enjoy as much freedom and exercise as a cat or dog.
