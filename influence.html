Further Reading

If you dig Neuromancer, here are the next 3 books you should read.

Snow Crash by Neal Stephenson
Hiro Protagonist goes from being a computer savvy ninja slinging pizzas to fighting for the freedom of millions. Also, there's a scrappy girl with jet rollerblades.
Count Zero by William Gibson
More Gibson, just do it.
The Diamond Age by Neal Stephenson
It starts slow but ramps up with ideas about a Neo-Victorian society in a world of nan-technology. When information can literally be pulled from teh air around us, what does it mean? Bonus: This one predicted the iPad.
