ZACH NILES

PRODUCER / DIRECTOR

Zach Niles is an award winning filmmaker and the guiding force behind Lakou Mizik. His documentary film, Sierra Leone's Refugee All Stars, won awards at 12 international film festivals and under Zach's management, the musical stars of the film have gone on to produce three critically acclaimed albums and continue to tour internationally. Zach is the co-founder and associate director of We Own TV, a film and multi-media educational program for disadvantaged youth in Freetown, Sierra Leone and served as interim director for Cine Institute, a film school in Jacmel Haiti. He has worked on the production and promotion of major music tours by artists such as Paul McCartney, The Rolling Stones, and Paul Simon.

NICOLAS JOLLIET

DIRECTOR OF PHOTOGRAPHY & EDITOR

Nicolas Jolliet has spent the past twenty years traveling and hunting for pictures, sounds and stories. Whether searching for the best Oud maker in Kurdistan, recording reggae musicians at his recording studio in St. Lucia or filming the rat eating tribes on the Nilgiri hills in India, Nico thrives on telling stories and recording music in the most fascinating places in the world.
