In partnership with the Artists Institute of Haiti, Lakou Mizik will provide free and low cost recording for local musicians and offer a variety of training opportunities for Haitian artists and producers.

Lakou Mizik will operate as a Fair Trade record label, creating sustainable income for artists through international online promotion, distribution and sales of their music.
