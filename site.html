LakouMizik.com will serve as a dynamic portal to host the rich media content produced throughout the project and will be an online marketplace for Haitian music. Built as a multimedia musical map, the site will be a window into a rarely seen side of Haiti and a place to discover the vibrant cultural traditions and new rhythms that emanate from dance clubs and taxi's throughout the country.

Through this interactive platform, the Lakou Mizik fan base will be able to connect directly with the artists & organizations that are featured on the site, offering opportunities for both support and collaboration. LakouMizik.com will also engage the community through outlets such as musical remix competitions and a dedicated space for users to share their own sounds, stories and photos from Haiti.
